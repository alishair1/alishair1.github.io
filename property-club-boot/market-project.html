Country Homes are elegant and yet simple. It represents a peaceful agrarian life, but at the same time it serves as a home base for its activity, hence connected to productivity. Country Homes can be seen as refuge, a safe environment that is grounded in purity, honesty, and simple value. Country Homes also gives its inhabitants a way to be somewhat self-sufficient.
Country Homes Farm Houses can easily be expanded because their basic shapes make it easy to add porches, sheds, and wings. These houses can also be dressed up or dressed down, depending on what you expect from a home. Each house takes on the imprint if the people who lived there, resulting in a truly vernacular style, one that adapts to local needs and preferences.
With the infrastructural growth in Pakistan, the vertical expansion of buildings is also somewhere causing asphyxiated state in terms of peace. Away from the hustle bustle of urban areas, people have started to invest in land in leess populated areas. There is a sudden rush or surge in investments on farm houses due to cramped surroundings of modern urban Pakistan. People prefer farmhouses to experience comfort in a second home, wherein they can also discover the joys of a country life.
Farmhouses are not just being used for residential purposes, but some owners today, are also using it for commercial purposes. Renting it out for wedding parties, corporate events, etc. is the current trend.As an investment option, any day, a farmhouse has its intrinsic value intact and appreciation of value as lucrative as land within city, or marginally less. The owner has the luxury of part disposal of larger chunk of farm land. It gives the farm owner flexibility of retaining the essentials and disposing extra unused for better ROI (Return on Investment).
