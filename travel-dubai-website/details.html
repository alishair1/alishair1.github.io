Half-day Dubai City Sightseeing Tour from Airport

The Morning Desert Safari Dubai starts from 7 am in the morning and opened for the whole day. The activities of day and night are different, which means you can easily enjoy most of the activities and games during the daytime.

With the beautiful view of sunrise on the dunes with your partner and family, you can enjoy a refreshing start to the day. Surely, it is the best experience to watch the sun coming out of the sandy and velvety Arabian mountains.

With children, a morning desert safari is always an adventure. You find this morning safari here with a clean start of the day which is missing at other places. We feel inner peace due to attractiveness and closeness with natural beauty. You can take your breakfast along with you and enjoy your meal with loved ones in the middle of sand dunes. It is best to visit from September to March as the temperature rises to almost 41 F.
